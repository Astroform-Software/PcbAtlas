# Localization Feedback Guide

Localization feedback helps PCB Atlas feel natural for engineers, manufacturing users, and reviewers in each language.

## What To Report

- Mistranslated PCB, manufacturing, import, export, or viewer terminology.
- Awkward UI wording.
- Text that is too long for the visible control.
- Untranslated strings.
- Inconsistent terms across the app and documentation.
- Region-specific terminology differences.

## Good Localization Report Shape

1. Language or locale.
2. Current text.
3. Where it appears.
4. Suggested replacement.
5. Why the replacement is better.

If a term has multiple valid translations, mention the region, toolchain, or professional context where your suggestion is common.

## Style Preferences

- Prefer terms that PCB designers, manufacturing engineers, and reviewers recognize quickly.
- Preserve established product names such as `PCB Atlas`, `Workbench`, and `Quick Estimate` unless the app has already localized them.
- Avoid overly casual wording in engineering and manufacturing flows.
- Keep button and menu labels short enough for compact UI.
- Use consistent wording for repeated concepts such as import, export, revision, layer, selection, issue, and estimate.

## Privacy

Screenshots for localization issues can still reveal private boards, file paths, or account details. Redact sensitive areas before posting.
